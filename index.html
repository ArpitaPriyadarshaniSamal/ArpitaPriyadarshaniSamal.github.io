I have always been keen for practical experiences. In my industrial training journey at SAIL, Rourkela and Aditya Aluminium & Power Plant, Lapanga I could explore and gain experience on practical challenges at industry.

During my Masters at IIT Jodhpur my project was based on thermodynamics calculation using Thermo-Calc software (CALPHAD approach) which was applied for Casting and Microstructure evolution prediction.

Further for my PhD in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at IIT Madras and Research Internship at Center of Excellence for Light Weighting, Sundaram Clayton Limted, Chennai, I delved to expand my research interest in Phase Field Modelling and Microstructure development via ICME Approach for Casting & Solidification process of Aluminium alloys.
